The sky darkened as the storm approached, casting an eerie glow over the landscape. The wind picked up, whistling through the trees and sending leaves scattering in all directions. The first drops of rain began to fall, heavy and cold, as thunder rumbled in the distance. The air was charged with electricity, and the scent of ozone filled the nostrils. People hurried to find shelter, their footsteps echoing through the suddenly deserted streets. Lightning flickered across the sky, illuminating the clouds with a brilliant, otherworldly light. The storm was coming, and with it, a sense of both dread and exhilaration.
